Different membership classes have different contributions to the membership total.

Each household member counts as 2 individual members, a corporate member counts as 4, a lifetime member counts as 1. The exact contribution from each member can be edited in the members section.

'Residence' members are a special case: 199 Knightsbridge has a single membership but counts as 100 individual members. Number of members can be edited in the 'Subscription' page.
